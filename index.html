What Are Palm Leaf Plates And Why Should You Care?

Areca palm leaf plates are an alternative to conventional disposable plates. They are stylish, elegant, and natural.

The palm sheaths from the areca nut trees fall off. They fall off of the trees in a natural way; the trees aren't cut.

We collect these dry, fallen leaves from the floor, and make pressed palm leaf plates. These plates don't pollute the environment because they are biodegradable. It is a renewable resource.
